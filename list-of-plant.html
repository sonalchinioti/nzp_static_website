LIST OF PLANTS OF NZP

A compilation of the plants of NZP was made in 1983 by Shri C. K. Warman. It included all categories of plants i.e herbs, shrubs, climbers and tree and a total of 405 species were described by him from the zoo. The same was further revised during 2008 taking the help of Dr. H. B. Naithani, a renowned taxonomist. Details are as follows:
