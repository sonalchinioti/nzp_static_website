VISITOR AMENITIES

NZP is visited by more than 27 lakhs visitors annually. A number of visitor facilities have been provided in NZP which is a continuous process and a few are:

  • Canteen (Outside)
  • Battery operated trolley
  • Dustbins
  • Parking facilities
  • Way finding Board & Directional signages
  • Drinking water facilities and washroom facilities
  • Souvenir shop
  • Signages
  • Cloakroom
  • Resting facilities
  • Guide map
  • ATM facility
  • Keepers talk
  • Wheel chair for physically challenged persons.

Proposed Visitor facilities

A new visitor centre is proposed which will consist following facilities:

  1. Education centre (Gallery, Activity Hall, Amphitheater, and Auditorium)
  2. Food Court
  3. Souvenir shop (work completed)
  4. ATM (Work Completed)
  5. Public Conveniences (completed a lot more In progress)
  6. Parking
  7. Forecourt to main entry
  8. Lawns
  9. Ticketing office
  10. Control Room (Security) and its rest room (Part completed)
